# Trusted Sources

NHS
The NHS (National Health Service) contains information on managing and expecting your NHS.

The Verity
The Verity is a charity that offers information and support to anyone living with PMOS.
www.verity-pcos.org.uk

The Lowdown
The Lowdown is a website to search and share PCOS experiences.
www.thelowdown.com

British Fertility Society
Information for the public about fertility
www.britishfertilitysociety.org.uk

ZOE
Information about diet and gut health.
www.zoe.com

British Dietetic Association (BDA) 
Information about diet and PMOS
